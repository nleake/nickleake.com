About Nick

Nick Leake is a DMV-based web designer specializing in illustration and typography. He currently works with the U.S. Treasury to create inclusive apps, websites, and design systems. He is also a part-time UX instructor at MICA. His work blends product management, digital media, and human-centered design.

Nick has served various industries including government, healthcare, education, finance, and journalism. He led a team modernizing refugee case management systems at USCIS. He also designed features for a faster publishing platform at the USA TODAY Network. He earned his degree in film from James Madison University, minoring in creative writing and biology.

Nick is passionate about art and technology that builds bridges instead of walls. He is particularly interested in work for public education, social justice, and environmental conservation. He's not interested in projects supporting defense, surveillance, crypto, or fossil fuels.
